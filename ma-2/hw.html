If you are feeling swamped with homeowrk then you can use these startegies to help

The first strategy to help with homework stress is to piece it out. Don't think about it as a whole break each class up and jus think about it one at a time.

Once youve broken up each class then do the assignemnt for that class. Once youve done that think of that as a win and you need to keep racking them up until you get the utlitmate win of finsihing your homework.
