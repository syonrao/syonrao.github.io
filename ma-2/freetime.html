To manage mental health it's beneficial to take breaks and practice self care

Free time provides an opportunity to decompress, allowing the mind to relax and recharge. It's during these moments that individuals can engage in self-care activities such as exercise, meditation, hobbies, or spending time with loved ones. These practices not only reduce stress and anxiety but also foster a sense of balance and resilience in the face of life's challenges. Neglecting self-care and free time can lead to burnout, heightened stress levels, and a decline in mental well-being.

There are a wide array of different method to practice self care to reduce stress levels. This links talks about some of the self care activites.
