Navigating College could be a stressful time consuming endeavor.

If you are struggling with stress or mental health, don't hesitate to seek professional help. Many colleges and universities offer counseling services for students. Talking to a counselor or therapist can provide a safe space to talk through your feelings and develop coping strategies.

Taking breaks during the college application process is crucial as it allows students to recharge, reduce stress, and maintain their overall well-being. The college application journey can be intense, with numerous tasks, deadlines, and high expectations. Breaks, whether short walks, moments of relaxation, or engaging in enjoyable activities, provide a mental and emotional respite. These pauses allow students to return to their tasks with increased focus, creativity, and a refreshed perspective. By taking regular breaks, students can better manage the stress and pressures that often accompany the college application process, ensuring a more balanced and healthier experience overall.
