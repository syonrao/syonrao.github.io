What we do

Here at Cantinetta we specialize in handmade delicacies from the Tuscan region of Italy. We make all of our pasta by hand, and all of the ingredients in our dishes are locally sourced and organic. We take pride in the care we have for our food. We ensure that each dish is seasoned and cooked to perfection.
